# Main Ideas
 
Device: Fitbit Versa 2  
Data available: heart rate, resting heart rate, sleep, steps, distance, calories, exercise sessions  


## 1. Malnutrition Risk & Eating Behavior Monitor
**SDG 2 – Target 2.2: End all forms of malnutrition**

### Concept
The app detects potential malnutrition or inadequate dietary intake using physiological signals rather than manual food logging.

### Wearable signals used
- Resting heart rate
- Daily activity
- Sleep quality
- Calorie expenditure

### Features
- Daily energy balance dashboard
- Alerts for possible undernutrition
- Weekly malnutrition risk score

### Example insight
“You burned 700 kcal today but showed no recovery signals. Consider reviewing your nutritional intake.”

### Why it is original
Most nutrition apps rely on manual food logging. This app estimates nutritional risk passively using biosignals.

## 2. Stress & Burnout Early Detection App 
**SDG 3 – Target 3.4: Promote mental health and well-being**

### Concept
Detect chronic stress and burnout patterns using wearable biomarkers.

### Indicators
- Increased resting heart rate
- Reduced sleep duration
- Reduced activity variability

### Core outputs
- Burnout risk index
- Recovery score
- Stress trend over time

### Example suggestion
“Your recovery score has decreased for 4 consecutive days. Consider reducing workload or improving sleep.”

## 3. Sleep Deprivation Cognitive Risk Predictor
**SDG 3 – Target 3.4**

### Concept
Predict cognitive impairment due to insufficient sleep.

### Data used
- Sleep duration
- Sleep consistency
- Resting heart rate

### Outputs
- Predicted attention performance
- Fatigue warnings
- Safety alerts
